Cookie Policy Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how MiraBath may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, and similar technologies on this website. MiraBath is an independent bathroom remodelling provider-matching platform and does not directly provide bathroom remodelling services.

Cookies may help the website function correctly, remember visitor choices, understand website usage, improve user experience, and support security or spam-prevention features.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Similar technologies, such as local storage or session storage, may also store information in your browser.

Cookies can be temporary and disappear after you close your browser, or they can stay on your device for a longer period depending on their purpose and your browser settings.

Types of Cookies This Website May Use

MiraBath may use several categories of cookies or similar technologies, including:

  • Essential cookies: Used for basic website functionality, navigation, security, form behaviour, cookie consent storage, and spam reduction.
  • Preference cookies: Used to remember choices such as cookie banner actions or interface preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand general website usage, page activity, referral sources, and performance trends.
  • Marketing or measurement cookies: May be used to understand campaign performance or improve relevance of website content where applicable.

Not every cookie category may be active at all times. Actual cookie use may depend on the website setup, analytics tools, advertising tools, and visitor preferences.

Cookie Consent Banner

The MiraBath website may display a cookie consent banner that allows visitors to accept or decline non-essential cookies. The banner is designed to remain visible until a visitor takes action.

If you accept cookies, the website may store that preference in your browser. If you decline cookies, non-essential cookie usage may be limited where technically supported.

Analytics and Website Measurement

MiraBath may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use the website. These tools may collect information such as pages visited, time on page, device type, browser type, approximate location, referral source, and interaction patterns.

Analytics information is generally used to improve website structure, page content, performance, usability, and provider-matching request flow.

Third-Party Tools

Some cookies or similar technologies may be provided by third-party services used for analytics, security, hosting, embedded scripts, form handling, advertising measurement, or website performance.

Third-party providers may process data according to their own policies. MiraBath does not control every technical behaviour of third-party scripts or browser-level tracking settings.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies in several ways:

  • Use the cookie banner when it appears on the website.
  • Change your browser settings to block, delete, or limit cookies.
  • Use private browsing modes or browser-level tracking protection tools.
  • Review settings from analytics or advertising providers where available.

Blocking cookies may affect website functionality, including saved preferences, request form behaviour, or other interactive features.

Relationship to the Privacy Policy

Cookies may be connected to information described in the Privacy Policy. Review that policy for more details about how submitted information and website usage information may be collected, used, or shared.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

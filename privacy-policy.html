Privacy Policy Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how MiraBath may collect, use, process, and protect information submitted through this website. MiraBath is an independent bathroom remodelling provider-matching platform and does not directly perform bathroom remodelling, installation, inspection, repair, or construction services.

By using this website or submitting a request form, you acknowledge that your information may be used to handle your request, improve website functionality, and help connect you with available participating provider options where applicable.

Information We May Collect

MiraBath may collect information that you voluntarily submit through forms, links, phone interactions, or email communication. This may include:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and general contact details.
  • Bathroom remodelling category, project type, timing goals, and request details.
  • Service area, general location, or other information needed to route a request.
  • Messages, comments, or additional details you choose to provide.
  • Consent confirmations connected with request submission.

This website may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, IP-related data, pages visited, referral source, cookie preferences, and general website usage activity.

How Information May Be Used

Information submitted through the website may be used for purposes related to provider matching, request handling, website operation, communication, and legal or security needs.

  • To receive, review, and process bathroom remodelling provider-matching requests.
  • To communicate about submitted requests or related service categories.
  • To help route request details toward available participating provider options.
  • To maintain website functionality, security, analytics, and spam prevention.
  • To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, or operational requirements.

Submitting a request does not create a service agreement with MiraBath or any participating provider.

How Information May Be Shared

MiraBath may share submitted request information with participating providers, service partners, support vendors, or operational service providers when reasonably necessary to handle a request or operate the website.

Participating providers are independent from MiraBath. Any final quote, schedule, warranty, inspection, service agreement, licensing information, insurance information, or work terms must be reviewed directly with the provider before hiring.

MiraBath may also disclose information when required to comply with law, protect website security, prevent fraud or misuse, enforce website terms, or respond to lawful requests.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, or similar technologies to support site functionality, remember cookie preferences, understand website usage, and improve user experience.

For more information, review the Cookie Policy. You may also adjust cookie settings through your browser controls.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a request form. You may also contact MiraBath using the contact details below to ask about submitted information, request correction of inaccurate information, or ask questions about this Privacy Policy.

  • You can use browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • You can avoid submitting optional project details that are not needed.
  • You can contact MiraBath about privacy-related questions.

Some website features may not work correctly if cookies or required form information are disabled.

Security

MiraBath may use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational measures to help protect submitted information. However, no website, email transmission, or internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Do not submit highly sensitive information through the contact form. The form is intended for general bathroom remodelling request details and provider-matching communication.

Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking bathroom remodelling provider-matching information. MiraBath does not knowingly request or collect personal information from children.

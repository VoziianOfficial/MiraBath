QUESTIONS

Bathroom matching questions

Clear answers about how MiraBath works as an independent provider-matching platform.

Does MiraBath remodel bathrooms directly?

No. MiraBath is an independent bathroom remodelling provider-matching platform. The platform helps homeowners submit project details and compare available provider options. MiraBath does not perform bathroom remodelling directly.

What happens after I submit a request?

Your request details may be reviewed and routed toward available participating provider options. Any final pricing, scheduling, and service terms come from those providers. MiraBath does not perform bathroom remodelling directly.

Can I compare more than one provider?

Where available, you may review more than one provider option and decide whether to continue with a participating provider. MiraBath does not perform bathroom remodelling directly.

What affects bathroom remodelling pricing?

Pricing can depend on scope, bathroom size, materials, layout complexity, timing, provider availability, and final provider terms. MiraBath does not perform bathroom remodelling directly.

Should I verify licensing and insurance?

Yes. Homeowners are responsible for verifying that any hired provider has the required license and insurance for the work being performed. MiraBath does not perform bathroom remodelling directly.

Does submitting a request create a service agreement?

No. Submitting a request through MiraBath does not create a service agreement. Any agreement is made separately between the homeowner and provider. MiraBath does not perform bathroom remodelling directly.
